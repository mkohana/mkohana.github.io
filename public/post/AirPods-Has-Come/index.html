AirPods Has Come

Mon, Jan 2, 2017 Apple, AirPods

I receive AirPods today.

Some persons say AirPods has some troublesome tasks. But, I think it is a good product.

Good Points

AirPods connects to multiple devices easily. I use iPhone 7, SE, AppleTV, and iMac, and connect the AirPods to iPhone SE and iMac. First, I connects it to the SE, then switch to the iMac. It is a easy task. In addition, the AirPods connects to the last connected device automatically.

Sound Quality is so good. The most bluetooth headphones has a little poor quality. But, the AirPods has a good quality and so high sound resolution.

The calling Siri is so easy. It can call Siri on iMac(but I don’t use it).

Bad Points

AirPods connects to the last connected device. So, it connects to the iMac evenif I want to connect it to iPhone. Therefore, I re-connect it to the iPhone. Maybe, the iMac is the out of range, AirPods connects to iPhone automatically.

The another bad point is the controller, but it is a not bad point for me. AirPods cannot controll the volume and so on. But, Tokyo is so noisy and the volume controll is not needed for me.

Totally, AirPods is a good product. It is a light weight and simple design, and has a good sound quality. I’m worried about the lost it.
