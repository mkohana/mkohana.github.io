I receive [AirPods](http://www.apple.com/airpods/) today.

![AirPods Box](/img/airpods-box.jpg "AirPods box")

Some persons say AirPods has some troublesome tasks.
But, I think it is a good product.

## Good Points
AirPods connects to multiple devices easily.
I use iPhone 7, SE, AppleTV, and iMac,
and connect the AirPods to iPhone SE and iMac.
First, I connects it to the SE,
then switch to the iMac.
It is a easy task.
In addition, the AirPods connects to the last connected device automatically.

Sound Quality is so good. The most bluetooth headphones has a little poor quality.
But, the AirPods has a good quality and so high sound resolution.

The calling Siri is so easy.
It can call Siri on iMac(but I don't use it).

## Bad Points
AirPods connects to the last connected device.
So, it connects to the iMac evenif I want to connect it to iPhone.
Therefore, I re-connect it to the iPhone.
Maybe, the iMac is the out of range, AirPods connects to iPhone automatically.

The another bad point is the controller,
but it is a not bad point for me.
AirPods cannot controll the volume and so on.
But, Tokyo is so noisy and the volume controll is not needed for me.

![AirPods](/img/airpods.jpg "AirPods")

Totally, AirPods is a good product.
It is a light weight and simple design,
and has a good sound quality.
I'm worried about the lost it.
